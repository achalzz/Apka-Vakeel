# Project Brief: LegalAI - Personal Legal Rights Awareness Tool

## Project Overview

LegalAI is an AI-powered web application designed to democratize access to legal information and services. The tool helps individuals understand their legal rights by simply describing their situation, and generates professional legal documents tailored to their needs.

## Core Mission

Bridge the legal services gap by providing accessible, affordable, and immediate legal information and document generation capabilities to citizens who cannot afford traditional legal services.

## Problem Statement

- **92%** of low-income Americans receive inadequate or no legal help for civil legal problems
- Over **40%** of family law litigants appear in court without representation
- **32%** of the UK population had an unmet legal need in 2024
- Legal fees are prohibitively expensive for a broad spectrum of people
- This is a global challenge requiring innovative solutions

## Solution

An AI-powered platform that:
1. Analyzes user situations and provides legal rights information
2. Generates professional legal documents (all types)
3. Provides 24/7 access without appointments or high costs
4. Uses plain language - no legal jargon required from users

## Target Users

- Low and middle-income individuals with legal questions
- People facing legal issues who cannot afford attorneys
- Individuals needing legal documents but lack access to legal services
- Anyone seeking quick legal information and guidance

## Key Features

### 1. Legal Rights Analysis
- Users describe their situation in plain language
- AI analyzes and provides comprehensive information about:
  - Legal rights
  - Available options
  - Recommended actions
  - Important warnings
  - Next steps

### 2. Document Generation
- Support for all major document types:
  - Contracts (business, service, etc.)
  - Legal letters (demand, notice, formal correspondence)
  - NDAs (Non-Disclosure Agreements)
  - Wills (Last will and testament)
  - Power of Attorney
  - Lease Agreements
  - Employment Contracts
  - Invoices
  - Cease and Desist Letters
  - Complaint Letters
  - Settlement Agreements
  - Partnership Agreements

### 3. User Experience
- Modern, intuitive interface
- No legal knowledge required
- Instant results
- Downloadable documents
- Mobile-responsive design

## Success Metrics

- Number of users accessing legal information
- Documents generated
- User satisfaction
- Reduction in barriers to legal information access

## Constraints

- Must include appropriate legal disclaimers
- Cannot replace professional legal counsel for complex matters
- Must be accurate and reliable
- Should be accessible (free or low-cost)

## Future Enhancements

- User accounts and session history
- Subscription/payment system
- More document types
- Multi-language support
- Document editing capabilities
- Integration with legal aid organizations
- Chat interface for interactive guidance
